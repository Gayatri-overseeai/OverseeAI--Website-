The company

Built for the duration of the obligations our clients carry.

Oversee is a software company. Its platform maintains the carrier-owned definition of each insurance product and generates the artifacts required by the carrier’s core systems.

01 / Why the company exists

The product definition should outlast the platform that implements it.

An insurance product is a long-lived obligation. Once policies are issued, the carrier must service, renew, endorse, and account for them — and keep the systems that carry them operational — for years after the launch team has moved on.

Most of the cost of that obligation is translation. The business defines the product; every platform team then interprets it again in the language of its own stack, and maintains that interpretation indefinitely. Estates change through acquisitions, core replacements, and new channels — and the product knowledge becomes inseparable from the configuration used to implement it.

The Oversee Product Catalog removes the repeated translation. It structures rates, rules, coverages, forms, classifications, versions, and variations into a durable, carrier-owned definition, and generates what each target system needs. Downstream systems and AI reference the same product model rather than reconstructing it independently.
