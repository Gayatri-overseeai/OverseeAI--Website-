The firm

Built for the duration of the obligations our clients carry.

Oversee is a privately held firm working with a limited number of carriers at a time, in engagements led by senior people.

01 / Why the firm exists

An insurance product is a long-lived obligation. We treat it that way.

Once policies are issued, the carrier must service, renew, endorse, and account for them — and keep the systems that carry them operational — for years after the launch team has moved on.

Most of the cost of that obligation is translation. The business defines the product; every platform team then interprets it again in the language of its own stack, and maintains that interpretation indefinitely.

Oversee exists to remove the repeated translation. We structure the requirements that define a product into a durable, carrier-owned definition, and generate what each target system needs from it. The definition outlives any single platform — which is the point.
