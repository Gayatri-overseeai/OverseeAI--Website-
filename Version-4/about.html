The company

Oversee is a software company.

Its platform maintains the carrier-owned definition of each insurance product and generates the artifacts required by the carrier’s core systems.

01 / Why the company exists

The product definition should outlast the platform that implements it.

Insurance products remain in force long after the launch team has moved on. The carrier must continue to service policies, process renewals and endorsements, handle claims, and maintain the systems that carry the product.

Technology estates change through acquisitions, core replacements, new channels, and business-unit decisions. Product knowledge is repeatedly translated into each new platform and often becomes inseparable from the configuration and code used to implement it.

The Oversee platform maintains the product requirements as carrier-owned infrastructure. The Product Catalog structures rates, rules, coverages, forms, classifications, versions, and variations, then generates what each target system needs. Downstream systems and AI can reference the same product model rather than reconstructing it independently.
